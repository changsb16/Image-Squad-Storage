# AI FWD Squad Kickoff
## Advancing Media Experiences in Engage (Web + Mobile)

---

## Agenda

### 1. Meet & Greet
- Quick intros: name, role, one thing you're excited to work on
- Clarify DRI for decisions vs. contributors

---

### 2. Media Issues & Opportunities — Show & Tell
- Walk through scope numbered by priority
- Some things will run in parallel — set the expectation that we may not get to everything, and that's okay
- **"Why now"** — show current pain points live (grey borders, forced landscape, etc.) to ground the work

---

### 3. Define "Done"
- Is the initial milestone **Engage Dogfood**?
- Confirm what that means per platform (Web vs. Mobile)
- This anchors all the scope conversations that follow

---

### 4. Project Scope — Flags & Discussion

| # | Item | Flag |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | Image collage fixes (borders, aspect ratio, balance) | Quick win — good week 1 target |
| 2 | Reduce friction in media post flow | Confirm UX direction / product opinion |
| 3 | Communities parity | Dependency on above? |
| 4 | Compose + preview modernization | Design-led — needs spike |
| 5 | Vertical video in feed | Rendering pipeline — estimate early |
| 6 | Full-screen engagement (comments, reactions) | Significant surface — design spike before build |
| 7 | Smart cropping (AI) | **Highest risk** — spike first, don't commit until model story is clear |
| 8 | Tag people in photos | Privacy/permissions — flag for policy review |
| 9 | User-selected hero image | Contained, clear UX — good mid-sprint target |

---

### 5. Ways of Working
- Daily standups? — open to proposals on time blocks
- Share-out / demo rhythm — Engage post style?
- Squad definition — 100% allocated or split-attention? This directly shapes what's deliverable in 7 weeks
- One place for decisions (Slack, Jira, Notion)

---

## Post-Kickoff
- Written summary of decisions + owners + open questions
- Backlog groomed with week 1–2 tickets ready
- Success metrics — how do we know the media experience is actually better?
